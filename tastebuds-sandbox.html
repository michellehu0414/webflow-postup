Objectivity and Integrity

3 stars may mean below-average to one diner and average to another, thus some diners are skeptical of the objectivity of star ratings. Therefore, I created a standardized rating system with clear labels to mitigate subjective ratings. In other words, this aims to make sure that diners are on the same page on ratings.
