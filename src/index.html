A little about me...

I’m driven by my passion for creativity, problem-solving, and social impact. My love for design is founded on my life-long interests in the arts and social impact.

I'm committed to robust research for impactful, sustainable products, which shapes my design approach—creating meaningful change and innovation for people that maximize business and organizational goals.

In my free time, apart from spending quality time with friends, family, and my adorable black cat Luna, I enjoy watching stand-up comedy, traveling, tending to my houseplants, cooking, and playing the viola and piano.
