Iterations

For my final iteration phase, I applied the recommendations I made from my previous usability testing. Below were my primary goals:

  • Amplify the social component.
  • Enhance visual aids.‍
  • Ensure users fully understand the app's purpose by the end of onboarding.

Route 1

Route 1

My iterations aim to ensure that users complete the full onboarding flow and have a solid understanding of TasteBuds by the end without experiencing cognitive overload.

Animated splash screen

I created an animated splash screen to begin educating users about the app early on while creating an engaging first impression.

Streamlined account creation

To compensate for adding the step to add friends into the onboarding flow, I simplified the account creation process.

Enhanced introduction slideshow

To improve comprehension, I enhanced the visual aids, implemented animations, and trimmed down the introduction copy to make it more engaging and scannable.
